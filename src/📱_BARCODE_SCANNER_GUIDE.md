## üìä Understanding Barcodes

### Barcode Types Supported by the Camera:
1. **UPC** (Universal Product Code) - 12 digits
   - Example: 012345678905
   - Common on retail products

2. **EAN** (European Article Number) - 13 digits
   - Example: 5901234123457
   - Common on European products

3. **Code128** - Alphanumeric
   - Example: ABC-12345
   - Flexible format

4. **Code39** - Alphanumeric
   - Example: *CODE39*
   - Simple format

5. **QR Codes** - 2D barcodes
   - Can contain any text/data
